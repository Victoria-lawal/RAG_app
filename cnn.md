### **Summary of CNN (Cable News Network)**

#### **Overview**
CNN (Cable News Network) is a global leader in news and information, providing 24/7 coverage across multiple platforms. Founded in 1980 by Ted Turner, it was the first television channel to offer 24-hour news coverage and remains a major source of breaking news, analysis, and investigative journalism.

#### **Mission & Branding**
- **Mission Statement:** CNN aims to *inform, engage, and empower* audiences worldwide.
- **Social Media Presence:**
  - **YouTube:** Focuses on delivering news and information continuously.
  - **Facebook:** Engages users with news content, live videos, and interactive features.
  - **Instagram:** Highlights global perspectives with a strong emphasis on storytelling and visual journalism.

#### **Key Platforms & Engagement**
- **YouTube:** Primary platform for video news content, live updates, and documentaries.
- **Facebook:** Used for community engagement, live streams, and sharing news articles.
- **Instagram:** Focuses on visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes content, and breaking news updates.

#### **Audience & Reach**
- **Instagram:** Over **23 million followers**, with a strong presence in news and multimedia storytelling.
- **Global Influence:** CNN is recognized for its in-depth reporting, international coverage, and real-time updates.

#### **Additional Notes**
- CNN operates under **Warner Bros. Discovery** (formerly Time Warner).
- Known for its **breaking news coverage**, political analysis, and investigative journalism.
- Maintains a strong digital and social media presence to reach diverse audiences.
